Mortal Kombat




Synopsis:
An Action-packed story that many people who are already in love with the video-game version will relate to. Hunted by the fearsome warrior Sub-Zero, MMA fighter Cole Young finds himself some sanctuary at the temple of Lord Raiden, where he begins training with experienced fighters Liu Kang, Kung Lao and the rogue mercenary Kano, Cole prepares to stand with Earth's greatest champions to take on the enemies from the Outworld in a brutal high-stakes battle for the universe.




Age-Restriction: 18+
